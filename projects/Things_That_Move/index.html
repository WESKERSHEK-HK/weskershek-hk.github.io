For the Things That Move Ltd. Rebrand project, I took on the role of Web Designer, utilizing my skills in React.js, three.js, tailwind css, webpress cms api, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe After Effects, and Figma. The goal of the project was to revamp the company's brand image and enhance its online presence.

Through my expertise in web design and development, I created a visually stunning and user-friendly website that showcased the company's portfolio of interactive designs. Implementing cutting-edge technologies such as React.js and three.js, I incorporated captivating animations and interactive elements to engage visitors.

The impact of the rebranding project was significant. The new website garnered attention from industry professionals and potential clients, resulting in an increased popularity for Things That Move Ltd. within the interactive design industry. As a result, the company attracted high-profile clients, including Cathay Cargo and HSBC, solidifying its position as a leading player in the field.
