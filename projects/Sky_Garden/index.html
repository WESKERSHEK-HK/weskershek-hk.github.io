• Sky Garden

    Music & Light Show System

  • Client
    Hong Kong Internation Airport
    Nomura

  • Role
    Software Engineer Lead

As the software engineer lead for this project, I developed a sophisticated centralized control solution that orchestrates over 800 individual LED lights, immersive audio playback, and mist machines throughout the venue.

My system integrates all of these technical elements through a custom CMS that allows operators to precisely schedule lighting shows, adjust music playlists, and control mist outputs down to the second. This provides the flexibility needed to constantly refresh the passenger experience.

At the core is a network of programmable controllers that synchronize all lighting, audio and mist sequences across the massive installation. My software ensures split-second timing between hundreds of DMX-linked fixtures moving in choreographed patterns.
