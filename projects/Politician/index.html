Politician, an art game that delves into the complex world of politics in Hong Kong. As the sole artist and developer, I took on the role of creating every aspect of the project, from the captivating graphic design to the seamless user interface and interactive gameplay. With my skills in Adobe Illustrator, Unity, and Figma, I was able to bring this unique game to life.

Politician offers players a choose-your-own-adventure experience set in a resource management context, mirroring the political landscape of Hong Kong. Through thought-provoking scenarios and decision-making, players navigate the challenging terrain of political maneuvering.

What sets Politician apart is not only its thought-provoking content, but also the fact that I personally engineered every technical aspect of the game. From designing the visuals to programming the immersive mechanics, I was able to fully realize my creative vision for this project. By merging my artistic skills with my technical expertise, I aimed to create an engaging and impactful experience that sheds light on the intricacies of politics in Hong Kong.
