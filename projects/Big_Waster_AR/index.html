Big Waster AR is an innovative mobile AR application designed to promote the virtual character "Big Waster" from the Environmental Protection Department of Hong Kong. As the Full Stack Developer for this project, I played a pivotal role in designing and developing the entire application, harnessing my expertise in Full Stack Development, Unity Development, Figma, and Adobe Illustrator.

One of the key highlights of my contribution was the implementation of the AR functionality, which allowed users to seamlessly interact with the virtual character in their real-world surroundings. I also incorporated photo and video capture tools, enabling users to capture memorable moments with Big Waster and share them on social media platforms.

Another significant aspect of my role was integrating image scanning technology, which enabled users to scan specific environmental objects and surfaces to bring Big Waster to life. This immersive experience not only entertained users but also aimed to raise awareness about environmental protection in a playful and engaging manner.

By taking ownership of the technical aspects of the project, I successfully bridged the gap between the virtual and physical worlds, allowing users to experience the charm of Big Waster in their everyday lives. The Big Waster AR application has garnered positive feedback from users and has made a significant impact in promoting environmental awareness and sustainability in Hong Kong.
