Dream of Falling, a captivating VR animation that takes viewers on a mesmerizing journey through the realm of dreams. As the artist behind this project, I utilized my skills in 3D modeling and animation using tools like After Effects, Blender, and more to bring this immersive experience to life.

In collaboration with the client, who sought to explore the concept of dreaming through personal artwork, I embarked on the creative process of crafting a virtual reality experience that would transport viewers into the depths of their own subconscious.

With Dream of Falling, my objective was to capture the essence of the dream state by immersing viewers in a series of surreal and ever-shifting environments. Through the power of VR technology, users find themselves floating through vivid dreamscapes, witnessing the unpredictable and non-linear nature of dreams firsthand.

The impact of Dream of Falling lies in its ability to provoke introspection and insight into the psychological and symbolic dimensions of dreams. By creating an interactive space where viewers can explore and engage with their own subconscious landscapes, this VR animation offers a unique and thought-provoking experience that transcends traditional art forms.
