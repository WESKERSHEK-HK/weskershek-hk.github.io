• Another 19 ways to create or mute sound

    Technical Art Performance 2022

  • Role
    Embedded Software Engineer

Another 19 ways to create or mute sound is a captivating technical art performance that showcases autonomous robot cars. As the embedded software engineer for this project, my role was to design the electronic schematics and robotics systems for the cars. This involved developing advanced position tracking capabilities and programming the cars to move in perfect synchronization with one another.

By leveraging my expertise in embedded C, TCP, and autonomous device development, I was able to engineer the underlying technology that controlled the robots. This was crucial in bringing the artistic concept to life and ensuring a seamless performance. The audience was treated to a mesmerizing display of the robot cars moving in intricate patterns, creating or muting sound in unique and unexpected ways.

With over 3000 people in attendance, the impact of Another 19 ways to create or mute sound was undeniable. The audience was captivated by the seamless integration of technology and art, as well as the innovative use of autonomous robot cars. This project not only pushed the boundaries of technical art, but also showcased the possibilities of combining engineering and creativity to deliver a truly immersive experience.
