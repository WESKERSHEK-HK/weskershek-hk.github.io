For the Hong Kong Massage Delivery project, I served as a multimedia designer for Brand New Page Ltd. (HK). My role was to design the brand identity and create engaging visual content for their website. With my expertise in Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and Wix, I was able to bring their vision to life.

Starting from sketches, I developed a unique and memorable logo that captured the essence of their massage delivery service. The logo conveyed a sense of relaxation, professionalism, and convenience, which aligned perfectly with their brand values.

In addition to the logo, I also created compelling content for the website. Using Adobe Photoshop, I carefully curated high-quality images that showcased the benefits of their services and created a sense of tranquility for the visitors. I also utilized Wix to design and build the entire website, ensuring a seamless and user-friendly experience for potential customers.

The impact of my work on the Hong Kong Massage Delivery project was significant. By designing a visually appealing brand identity and creating an immersive website, I helped them establish a strong online presence and attract a larger customer base. The website became the go-to platform for individuals seeking massage services, offering a convenient and reliable solution for their relaxation needs.
