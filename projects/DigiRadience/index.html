DigiRadience, an immersive projection exhibit titled by artist Vvzela Kook & Ng Tsz Kwan, was a captivating experience that I had the privilege to be a part of as an animator. My role in this project involved designing and creating significant portions of the 3D animation that brought the exhibit to life.

Through my animations, I aimed to transform the audience's perception of the buildings by showcasing them from various angles, employing mesmerizing lighting effects, and even deconstructing their structures. The animations were meticulously crafted to technically display the architecture in a visually stunning and engaging manner, without relying on unnecessary embellishments.

The impact of DigiRadience was remarkable, as it attracted over 5000 visitors who were captivated by the immersive projection mapping experience. The combination of innovative animation techniques and the unique artistic vision of Vvzela Kook & Ng Tsz Kwan created a truly unforgettable and thought-provoking exhibition that left a lasting impression on all who experienced it.
