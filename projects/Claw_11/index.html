As a full stack developer, my role in this project was to create a captivating gaming experience for players, while also integrating essential features and functionalities.

With my expertise in full stack development, I took charge of building the entire game from scratch. This involved designing the gameplay mechanics, ensuring smooth user interactions, and implementing API integrations for issuing shopping coupons to the lucky winners.

By taking ownership of the technical aspects of the game, from its core design to the backend reward functions, Claw 11 delivered a seamless and enjoyable gaming experience that not only entertained the players but also helped drive the promotional goals of our esteemed client, K11 HK.

Claw 11 stands out as a unique and engaging virtual claw game, offering players the thrill of winning shopping coupons through their gaming skills. With its seamless integration of technology and user-friendly interface, this interactive experience has successfully captivated the attention of mall-goers, boosting footfall and enhancing the overall shopping experience. The combination of my full stack development skills, unity expertise, and API integrations has resulted in a truly immersive and rewarding experience for players, making Claw 11 a standout project in the realm of virtual gaming.
